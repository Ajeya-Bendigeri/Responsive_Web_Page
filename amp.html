Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing
Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

High-Precision Extrusion

Raw PE100 granules are melted and continuously pushed through a precision die, forming the pipe shape at exact diameter specifications.

  • Controlled melt temperature
  • Consistent wall thickness

Controlled Cooling Process

Pipes pass through calibrated water cooling baths that lock in pipe geometry and relieve internal stresses for long-term performance.

  • Uniform cooling rate
  • Stress-free pipe structure

Precision Vacuum Sizing

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • Tight OD tolerances (±0.1mm)
  • Roundness verified continuously

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes hydrostatic pressure testing, dimensional checks, and material composition validation before leaving the line.

  • ISO 4427 compliant testing
  • 100% hydrostatic pressure check

Inkjet Pipe Marking

Continuous inkjet printers mark each pipe with size, SDR rating, pressure class, batch number, and production date for full traceability.

  • BIS/ISO marking standards
  • Full batch traceability

Automated Length Cutting

Flying saw cutters automatically trim pipes to the exact specified length without stopping the line, maximising throughput.

  • ±5mm cutting accuracy
  • No production line downtime

Secure Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are bundled, end-capped, and strapped for safe transit. Each bundle is labelled with a QR code linking to test certificates.

  • UV-stabilised end caps
  • QR-linked test certificates
